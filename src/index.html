About me

A little about me. I love getting my hands dirty. For most of my life, that meant actual dirt. I have worked in the most beautiful outdoor spaces in the west as a guide taking people on outdoor adventures. Usually, close to or in National Parks from Alaska to the Rio Grande, I was actually getting my hands dirty.

One of the best parts of getting dirty in the outdoors is Mother Nature loves to throw curve balls your way at anytime. This is where I thrive. Using available resources to solve problems in a collaborative environment to reach a common goal while learning new ways to view a problem is my bread and butter. On a river, the saying goes, "Always look downstream." This means to address the issues at hand while setting up for success in the future. From a developer's standpoint, staying current with the direction the industry is going and writing code that can be adaptable to the next wave of advancement is imperative.

I love outdoor activities from whitewater rafting through the Grand Canyon to walking my dogs around the park. And coffee. I think in another life, I was a coffee bean farmer. I think for my next personal goal, I will learn to roast my own beans. Till then, I will let the professionals handle it.
